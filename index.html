Privacy Policy for AndIRide

Last updated: January 2026

AndIRide (“the App”) is an adventure navigation application designed to help users explore routes, discover places, and navigate outdoors. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data

The App may collect precise location data (GPS) while the App is in use. This data is required to provide core functionality such as:

  • Route building
  • Navigation
  • Map display
  • Outdoor exploration features

Location data is used only while the App is running and when location access is granted by the user.

Device and Technical Information

The App may automatically collect limited technical information, such as:

  • Device model
  • Android version
  • App version
  • Basic diagnostics for stability and performance

This information is used solely to improve app functionality and reliability.

2. How We Use Information

Information collected by AndIRide is used only to:

  • Provide navigation and routing features
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Ensure correct operation of the App

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share personal data with third parties.

Data is not shared with advertisers, data brokers, or analytics services beyond what is required for basic app functionality provided by the Android platform.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • AndIRide does not require user accounts
  • No personal profiles are created
  • Data is processed locally on the device or temporarily in memory as needed
  • Reasonable technical measures are used to protect data

5. User Control

You are always in control of your data:

  • Location access can be enabled or disabled via Android system settings
  • You can stop data collection by closing the App or revoking permissions

6. Children’s Privacy

AndIRide is not intended for children under the age of 13. The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
